KŌRERO '27

Kōrero '27 communicates a design exibition coming to Auckland in 2027. The event will host eight designers, featuring their work throughout the gallery. The Kōrero design captures a combination of the eight designers styles, creating a vibrant, playful display of post-modern elements. It's engaging forms open opportunity for common ground amongst designers and their audience.

THE BOTANIC TRUST

The Botanic Trust invites people from all around New Zealand to join the Botanic Family where they learn and grow together. It's brand identity captures the unique characteristics of intertwining and overlapping vines. Their strong bond formed with their surroundings promotes the connection between members of the Botanic Family.

Branding Design System Visual Identity

Terrafold

Terra-fold accommodates for people who are in a less financially stable position. The tiny-home folds into the natural forms of nature that in-turn creates a unique aesthetic. It breathe elegance and strengthens our relationship with what is natural and authentic. The design takes sustainably sourced material in the form of harbour rocks for the exterior, that when carved, reveals the 'riches' inside.

Exhibition Design Spatial Planning Visitor Experience

VINSACRÈ

Vinsacrè is a 0% alcohol wine brand that marked the beginning of my design journey. Created as my Level 2 high school project, it represents my first step into the world of design. Through Vinsacrè, I discovered both my strengths and the areas I needed to develop - insights that I carried forward into university, where I've continued refining my skills and working toward professional design outcomes.
